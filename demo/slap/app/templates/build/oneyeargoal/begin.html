Establishing a 1 year goal

Your one-year goal is a measurable financial goal for your business. It needs to be clearly stated and ambitious - but realistic. Pure and simple. Without this clarity, it is almost impossible to grow. By creating a one-year goal you always have a target to both focus on and benchmark yourself against.

Creating a one-year goal can be daunting, but we're going to break it out in 4 easy steps:

  1. Expenses – We'll identify the expenses of your business, both on the personal and company side.
  2. Revenue Streams – We'll help you break out and identify the major revenue streams of your business and label what type of impact each has on your bottom line.
  3. Costs – We'll look at the costs of operating your business, both fixed and variable.
  4. Breakeven – Based on the information above, we'll identify your true breakeven point to see where you're at today and where you think you can be a year from now.

Each phase is a big step in identifying your financial goal, but we'll walk you through – step by step – so you understand the process and it stays simple, actionable, and doable.
