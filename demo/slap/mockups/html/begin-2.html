Establishing a 1 year goal

This one-year goal is a clearly identified, aggressive, realistic, measurable financial goal for your business. Pure and simple. Without this clarity, it is almost impossible to grow. By creating a one-year goal you always have something to move towards and to measure your success.

Creating a one-year goal can be daunting, but we're going to break it out in 4 easy steps:

  1. Expenses – We'll identify the expenses of your business &em; both on the personal and company side.
  2. Revenue Streams – We'll help you break out and identify the major revenue streams of your business and label what type of impact each has on your bottom line.
  3. Costs – We'll look at the costs of your business, both fixed and variable.
  4. Breakeven – Based on the information above, we'll identify your true breakeven to see where you're at today and where you think you can be a year from now.

Each phase is a big step in identifying your financial goal, but we'll walk you through – piece by piece – so you understand the process and it stays simple, actionable, and doable.
