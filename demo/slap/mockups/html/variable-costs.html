Variable Costs

For each revenue stream, enter the variable costs of delivering that product. For example, if you sell shoes, you have the cost of manufacturing the shoes or buying them wholesale. And if you ship them to your customer, you have the cost of shipping. You might also have the commission you pay a salesperson. These are the costs that you only incur when you sell the product to your customer. Remember, you've covered your fixed expenses, your overhead, on a previous page.
