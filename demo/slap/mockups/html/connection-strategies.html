Connection Strategies Overview

The SLAP method consists of 4 quarters of action items, each tied to a particular strategy for reaching and engaging the business's ideal client. These are called "Connection Strategies", and the SLAP includes 8 to choose from. The idea is that each of these strategies connects your business to your ideal client. This page is going to take some time. We will review each connection strategy to provide you the information you need to make the best decision for your business.
