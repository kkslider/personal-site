Putting the "AP" in SLAP

In the steps leading up to this point, we've established your financial goal and who you need to target to get there. Now it's time to put some thought in what actions you can take to reach your goal. Remember, the SLAP is all about action - so let's get to work.

  1. First we're going to finalize the 12-month action plan.
  2. We'll review eight connection strategies and help you decide which connection is right for your business by quarter.
  3. We'll establish quarterly goals, so throughout the year, you'll be able to see if you're on track.
  4. We'll develop monthly action items, specific actions that, when executed in a timely fashion, are designed to help you achieve your quarterly goal.
  5. The monthly benchmarks will be set as the "yes or no" success points to measure each month.

You've come this far, let's get to work building the plan that will help you reach your goals. Are you ready to get to work?
